Booking Rules

Clean Up After Yourself:

Please ensure that you clean up any personal items, trash, or equipment after your session. Leave the area in the same condition as you found it to ensure a pleasant experience for everyone.

Arrive on Time:

Be punctual for your booking. Arriving on time ensures that everyone gets their full allotted time and helps maintain a smooth schedule.

Access Code:

The access code to the door will be sent to your email 10 minutes before your scheduled start time. Please check your email for this information to ensure you can enter the facility.

Pricing and Invoices:

The price for booking the court is $10 per hour. An invoice will be sent to your accounts email once your booking is confirmed. Payment must be made within 1 day after your session. Failure to pay the invoice will result in the suspension of your account, preventing further bookings until the outstanding balance is settled.

Borrowing Equipment:

Equipment can be borrowed from a box located inside the door. Please handle all equipment with care and return it to the box after use.
